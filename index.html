How It Works

Every conversion happens inside your browser's sandbox. When you drop a file, it stays on your device - the code reads it, transforms it in memory, and gives you the result. No server ever sees your data.

Images

Canvas API handles resize, rotate, flip, filters, and format conversion to PNG, JPG, or ICO.

Audio

Web Audio Context decodes, resamples, adjusts speed and volume, then exports to MP3 or lossless WAV.

Video

Captures frames, extracts audio tracks, or renders muted video - all in the browser.

Archives

Browse and extract ZIP contents locally without any third-party services.
